Tribreak

April 2019

A game I've been working on and off of for the past 2 years. I finally decided to rip off the bandaid and finish the damn thing, and this is the result. The game is a completely original concept, I didn't follow any stupid youtube tutorial series to make it. The game requires you to beat levels by stragically destroying all the triangles in a way that lets you land on other triangles. I designed all the textures, sounds, gameplay, code, and marketing by myself, which is kinda dope. I released it on android for free, so if you have an android phone you can download and play it!
